How to play

Every kid has three stats: strength, intelligence and assholiness.

Assholiness:
strong against type , weak against high stat.

Defend:

Schoolfight is a turn based game I created using jQuery, Vanilla JS and CSS. Pick a party of three characters and battle it out. The game uses a rock paper scissors principle with one type being more effetive then others. There is a total of nine levels including two mini games and a boss fight.
